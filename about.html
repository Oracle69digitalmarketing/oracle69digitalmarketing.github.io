Our Mission

Oracle69 Digital Marketing is dedicated to helping businesses of all sizes succeed online. Our team specializes in SEO, content marketing, and social media management.

Who We Are

Oracle69 Digital Marketing was founded by Adewumi Adewale, a seasoned digital strategist, software developer, author, and agripreneur. At Oracle69, we build smart, scalable marketing systems that empower rural and underserved businesses to grow and thrive in the digital space.

We go beyond typical agency work—offering intelligent automation, data-driven content strategy, and an all-in-one platform called the Harmony Marketing Hub. This AI-powered system unifies campaign management, automates workflows, and delivers real-time insights across all your channels.

What Makes Us Different

  • Rural-Focused Strategy: We tailor digital solutions to underserved markets with limited digital access.
  • Harmony Marketing Hub: Our proprietary tool integrates your marketing stack for effortless control and maximum ROI.
  • Full-Service Execution: From SEO to content writing to social media to web design—we deliver a complete, unified system.
  • Affordable, Scalable Plans: Packages are priced to support small businesses while enabling growth through automation and analytics.

About Our Founder

Adewumi Adewale operates at the intersection of marketing, technology, and sustainable agriculture. He is also the co-founder of Tessyfarm, an agritech venture focused on fish farming, smart feed systems, and eco-friendly fertilizer production. His work bridges the gap between rural business growth and modern digital innovation.

Through Oracle69 and Tessyfarm, Adewale champions tech-powered transformation—from data automation to transparent agri-supply chains—while mentoring entrepreneurs and deploying solutions that scale in low-resource environments.
